Biography

"Kat" Courtney is a pioneer in the modern day Plant Medicine space; she is a traditionally trained Ayahuasquera and Huachumera with over 2 decades of professional psychedelic experience. Kat apprenticed for over 10 years in the Shipibo-Conibo and Quechua-Lamista traditions. She's the author of Plant Medicine Mystery School Vol 1: The Superhero Healing Powers of Psychotropic Plants – a book that celebrates the dynamic and sentient personalities of all the entheogens.

Kat is a trailblazer in the Psychedelic Integration space, as well as an expert in cultivating life-changing relationships with plant spirits. She leads Master Plant Diets both in person and remotely, and is available for coaching about this and all shamanic topics. Kat is also a certified Death Doula honored to help people make peace with the inevitable and beautiful transition into the afterlife.

Speaking Topics & Audiences

Topics & Talking Points

The following questions are designed to spark meaningful, high-value conversations with Kat. Feel free to adapt them to your format or audience.

01 Kat apprenticed in traditional lineages for over a decade, including periods of isolation and drinking ayahuasca for 30 consecutive nights during her training — what was that experience like and how did it shape her work?
02 What is the difference between plant consciousness and human consciousness — ego-based survival vs. unity consciousness — and how can this shift quiet anxiety, addiction, and mental suffering?
03 What is ego dissolution actually (and isn't), why can chasing it be destabilising, and why does grounded integration matter more than peak experiences?
04 What is shadow work, and how does someone begin — with or without plant medicine?
05 How do you choose ethical, safe, culturally respectful plant medicine facilitators — and what are the red flags most people miss?
06 How did being born with a life-threatening breathing disorder and having a near-death experience in her 20s shape Kat's lifelong relationship with death and healing?
07 What do plant medicines teach about resistance and surrender — and how does that impact the way Kat approaches challenges in everyday life?
